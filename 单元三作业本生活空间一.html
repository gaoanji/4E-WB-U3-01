野兔泛滥

时间: 从19世纪初开始
shí jiān: cóng 19 shì jì chū kāi shǐ
Time: Starting from the early 19th century
国家/地区: 澳大利亚
guó jiā/dì qū: ào dà lì yà
Country/Region: Australia
起因: 外来人口大量 入澳大利亚, 野兔也被大量引进。
qǐ yīn: wài lái rén kǒu dà liàng [yǒng] rù ào dà lì yà, yě tù yě bèi dà liàng yǐn jìn.
Cause: A large number of foreign populations poured into Australia, and wild rabbits were also introduced in large numbers.
结果: 因环境适宜, 加上没有天敌, 野兔大量 , 在20世纪初期数量已超过100亿。野兔的急剧增加 成大祸: 草地被破坏; 牛羊的 来源受到影响; 生态系统遭到破坏。
jié guǒ: yīn huán jìng shì yí, jiā shàng méi yǒu tiān dí, yě tù dà liàng [fán zhí], zài 20 shì jì chū qī shù liàng yǐ chāo guò 100 yì. yě tù de jí jù zēng jiā [niàng] chéng dà huò: cǎo dì bèi pò huài; niú yáng de [sì liào] lái yuán shòu dào yǐng xiǎng; shēng tài xì tǒng zāo dào pò huài.
Result: Due to the suitable environment and the lack of natural enemies, wild rabbits reproduced in large numbers, exceeding 10 billion in the early 20th century. The sharp increase in wild rabbits led to a disaster: grasslands were destroyed; the source of feed for cattle and sheep was affected; the ecosystem was destroyed.
应对措施: 捕杀、引入天敌、运用生物技术等。
yìng duì cuò shī: bǔ shā, yǐn rù tiān dí, yùn yòng shēng wù jì shù děng.
Countermeasures: Culling, introducing natural enemies, using biotechnology, etc.

福寿螺造成严重问题

时间: 从1980年代开始
shí jiān: cóng 1980 nián dài kāi shǐ
Time: Starting from the 1980s
国家/地区: 中国
guó jiā/dì qū: zhōng guó
Country/Region: China
起因: 福寿螺作为食物被引入中国。
qǐ yīn: fú shòu luó zuò wéi shí wù bèi yǐn rù zhōng guó.
Cause: The Golden Apple Snail was introduced to China as food.
结果: 不但没有成为餐桌上的美味 , 反而在自然环境中泛滥成灾。
jié guǒ: bù dàn méi yǒu chéng wéi cān zhuō shàng de měi wèi [jiā yáo], fǎn ér zài zì rán huán jìng zhōng fàn làn chéng zāi.
Result: Not only did it not become a delicacy on the table, but it flooded into a disaster in the natural environment.
影响: 福寿螺 食稻苗, 给农业生产带来影响。
yǐng xiǎng: fú shòu luó [kěn] shí dào miáo, gěi nóng yè shēng chǎn dài lái yǐng xiǎng.
Impact: Golden Apple Snails gnaw on rice seedlings, affecting agricultural production.
应对措施: 将福寿螺列为外来 物种, 大力防治。
yìng duì cuò shī: jiāng fú shòu luó liè wéi wài lái [rù qīn] wù zhǒng, dà lì fáng zhì.
Countermeasures: List the Golden Apple Snail as an invasive alien species and vigorously prevent and control it.
